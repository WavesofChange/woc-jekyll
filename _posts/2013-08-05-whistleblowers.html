--- layout: post title: "Whistleblowers" author:Raliat Akesode date: 2013-08-05 categories: news --- Ever wonder what a whistleblower is? A whistleblower is an informant who exposes wrongdoing within an organization in the hope of stopping it. Two recent world wide known whistleblowers would be Eric Snowden and Bradley Manning who exposed information to the public. Eric Snowden
