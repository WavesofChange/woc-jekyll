Rushaine from Levo League

On August 22 we hung out with Rushaine McBean from The Levo League. She informed us on her career and history using the computer. Rushaine also talked about her experience going through college as a computer science major. She stressed that although the journey was difficult, in the end it worked out for the best; now she is able to do what she loves for a living.
