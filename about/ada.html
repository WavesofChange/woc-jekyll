Ada Gonzalez

My name is Ada Gonzalez. I was born on February 14, 1996. Yes, that’s right, I’m 17 years old. I was born and raised in New York City; I guess that’s why I love the city. I have two sisters: one is older than me and the other is younger than me which makes me the middle child.

I am proud to say that I come from a Mexican family. I attend school at Life Sciences Secondary School located on the East side in which I’m in my last year of High School. School is pretty fun; my favorite subjects are Art, Drama and English. I also participate in my school's soccer team. I enjoy talking and hanging out with my friends. I like to listen to music and watch movies on my free time. I love food; My favorite foods are pasta and tacos.
