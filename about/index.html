About Waves of Change

Waves of Change West Harlem Project began mid-July and is a 6-week internship that develops and builds skills in writing, web development, photography, and videography through documenting local community media. Interns meet twice a week, each session is five hours. Interns attend local tech and cultural events; they have had workshops from leaders and mentors in Harlem and entrepreneurial women of color; they have produced blog and project entries; and they've collaborated with other CD9 organizations. Their work and progress can be viewed at harlemny.deepdishwavesofchange.org.
