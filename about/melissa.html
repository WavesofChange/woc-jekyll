Melissa Hernandez

Here goes,

I'm Dominican-American, but I am also many things. I’m a writer, couldn’t do much with feelings if I couldn’t write, since I love writing short stories and poems and spoken words. I'm an actor, I love theatre. I'm also a guitarist and a singer and songwriter. I'm gonna be a Christian rock star, don't doubt it.

I have so many dreams that sometimes I honestly cannot comprehend how I’ll deal with them. The ultimate life goal for me is to become a rock star, and it is no joke. Since a child in elementary school I’ve taken up the guitar, and I’ve been singing since forever. There is nothing I’ve wanted more than to live by the music that God’s blessed me with. I feel I’ve lived so much in this world, with the knowledge and education and the work experience I’ve received with the several jobs & internships I’ve been able to work in. I feel lucky to be able to embrace what it is to be blessed with so many opportunities and gateways.

I’ve been constantly moving since I was a child, but everywhere I go, I strive to make an impact with my personality, leadership, friendship, and loyalty. I recently graduated from City-As High School in June 2013. For my post education goals, I will be attending Bronx Community College as a undergraduate freshman this fall, in Bronx, New York. I plan to work up to transferring to a university or college in the south of the United Kingdom. It’s been my goal to go to England since the beginning of high school, and now I’m only a few steps away.

Interning at City-As High School, I got to experience oh so many different environments and moments. My first internship was at WPLJ 95.5 FM as a promotional assistant. After that I interned at Barbara’s Flowers as a Florist’s assistant. My penultimate internship was at Shining Hope for Communities as an administrative assistant intern. My last internship was at The Horticultural Society of New York as a special events intern. Right now, I'm working at Waves of Change as an intern and hoping this can be a very fulfilling internship for me. All these experiences added together were a humongous help to my career goals, although they may not exactly be direct, they’ve definitely been a great insight and preview of some of the things that I might want to have a taste in. As a result of all these internships I do feel more suited for the what is considered to be the “real world” because of the experience and new skills I’ve gained and I’m more likely to be a better fit for certain jobs because of these experiences.

To conlude, I LOVE Japanese Pop-Culture, Manga, Fashion, Cosplay; Anything with any sort of anime flare is in my love list. Ex: Sanrio, Gothic Lolita, Anime, you name it! Video games like Final Fantasy also hold an extremely special place in my heart. Reading all sorts of books, comedy, and skyping are just a few of the things I love to do just for the gist of who I am.

See ya on the flipside,

Melissa
