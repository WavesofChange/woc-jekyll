Collaborations

Throughout the summer, Waves of Change Interns collaborated with various West Harlem Organizations. The focus of the project was to create and support. We focused on work with other organizations that had received funding through WHDC.

Def Dance Jams

Founded 20 years ago by Sister Aziza, Def Dance Jams provides community members with mixed abilities the environment for creative expression through dance, movement, and sign language.
