Mist Harlem

My Image Studios LLC is the owner and operator of MIST Harlem, which includes Harvist Restaurant. They are a fair trade social venture planned, programmed and built to share the authentic and diverse cultures of the African and Latino diaspora. Though culturally specific, the stories – whether told through food, film, dance, spoken word or music – are about universal themes that bind us all together.
