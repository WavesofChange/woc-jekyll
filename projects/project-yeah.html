Young Empowered Artists of Harlem, or Y.E.A.H., is a youth visual arts initiative involving 20 youth from West Harlem Community District 9 that creates public art in West Harlem within an education context. The interns will create an art project on pertinent topics on history, social issues, technology, cultural differences, the humanities and the sciences.

Summer 2013 program focused on stopping violence in the community.
