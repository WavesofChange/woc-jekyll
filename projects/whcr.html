WHCR

As a community radio station broadcasting from the campus of the City College of New York, WHCR seeks to provide skills in communications and related disciplines to students here at the college and community members. WHCR seeks to empower it's listeners by providing informative, educational and cultural programming that speaks to the diverse populations of Harlem, upper Manhattan; and some sections of the Bronx, Queens, and New Jersey.
